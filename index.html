My Music

How I Got Started

Originally, I had started my music journey taking singing lessons. That only lasted about six months before I had quit. About a year and a half after that, my mom told me that I should have a creative outlet as a way to channel my emotions in a healthy way. My first instrument was ukelele, but it seemed too easy after a year, or so. Then I'd started guitar. My sister had started playing years ago, so I felt comfortable enough. Playing guitar felt like I was in my sister's shadow even more than before, so I stopped playing.

The reason I stared bass, though, is a little funny. When COVID hit, me and my family got really close. Especially me and my sister, CeCe. In the fall of 2021, CeCe started playing music at public events, but she had said something felt off about the sound. Since I already played a couple instruments, she decided that I would be her personal bassist.
